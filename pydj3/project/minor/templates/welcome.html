ABOUT US

We Love Technology

At HAWKEYE PATROL, we provide services to our clients to help them against any such malpractices and advice them on strategic methods to reduce cyber attacks on their organisations. Our blogs also help interested readers know more about trending topics in this industry, keeping them on their toes on latest trends, informative guides and descriptive tutorials on ethical hacking.

HAWKEYE PATROL, established in 2022, was an idea put forward by like minded people who wanted the general public to know more about digital crimes. Created in a small room, our website aims towards helping people reduce their chances of being robbed of their private digital properties. Our game is to reach out to the general public and sharing our knowledge of the cyber security world to them.

We care about our peers, both young and old, and threats that come across them. We strive to make this world a better place, a safe place where everyone gets their own share of unlimited privacy.
