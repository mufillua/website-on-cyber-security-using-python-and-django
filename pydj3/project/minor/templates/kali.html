GET TO KNOW KALI LINUX & ITS TOOLS

What is Kali Linux?

Kali Linux launched in 2013 by the organisation Offensive Security, is one of the most important tools that Cyber Security professionals, or more specifically professional penetration testers swear by. Kali Linux was especially built for its use in data forensics and penetration testing. This Debian-based version of Linux can be downloaded for free, and used in various cyber-security practices such as exploitation and many more!

The best thing about Kali Linux is the 600+ pre-installed tools that come with it! These tools are used in various penetration testing and for other cyber-security activities. Let us look at some of the tools that Kali Linux has to offer us:



Some Tools Available With Kali Linux

  • JOHN THE RIPPER

    • This tool is a very effective for cracking passwords. Cyber security professionals use the various combinations of cracking modes offered by it. It can even be used to crack encrypted passwords, or zipped files using techniques such as Brute force or Dictionary attack.

  • SOCIAL ENGINEERING TOOLKIT

    • We have come across the term "phishing" every now and then. Generally in a phishing attack, confidential information such as bank details, passwords are taken from the victim on the pretext of being a legitimate website, which ultimately leads to their money and identity being stolen. Social Engineering tools available with Kali Linux, can be used to perform such attacks. It gathers information about user-behaviour, thus making it a great tool for performing phishing attacks.

  • METASPLOIT

    • Metasploit is the most popular penetration testing framework. This open-source tool is widely used to exploit vulnerabilities present over operating systems and networks. It is a favourite among cyber security specialists as it can be used to discover vulnerabilities and assess risks, thereby enabling experts to take measures to improve security.
